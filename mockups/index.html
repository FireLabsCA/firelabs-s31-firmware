Power calibration
The CSE7766 ships uncalibrated. Calibrate once against a known load.
You'll need a plug-in power meter (Kill-A-Watt or similar) and a steady load such as an incandescent bulb or a space heater on a fixed setting.
  1. Plug the meter and the load into this outlet, relay ON.
  2. Let the reading settle for ~30 seconds.
  3. Type the meter's true watts below and hit Calibrate.
